INTRODUCTION

2001年生，来自台湾，同济大学设计创意学院工业设计本科在读。在设计上，我偏爱从特定的社会话题出发进行设计思考，注重人文关怀和情感传递。我敏感、细致、且富有创造力，擅长沟通与表达，常常通过故事感的方式传达设计作品。

我认为设计是一种我与理想世界的沟通方式，我通过设计作品表达我的观念，我对世界的理解和我对人的关怀。

I was born in 2001 and I come from Taiwan. I am studying at the Design And Innovation College at Tongji University, major in industrial design. In design, I prefer to start design thinking from specific social topics, focusing on humanistic care and emotional transmission. I am sensitive, meticulous, and creative. I am good at communication and expression and I love to convey design works through a sense of story.

I think design as a way of communicating with my ideal world. I express my ideas, my understanding of the world and my care for people through design works.
